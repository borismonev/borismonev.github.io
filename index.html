Welcome to my website!

My name is Boris and this is my first ever website, and I am super excited to share it! Here you can find information about me, my journey progress in the ICT programme, frequently asked questions and my blog page!

Why I chose ICT:

I decided to study ICT because of my interest in technology as a whole. I love experimenting with various devices, seeing how they work, and experiencing the innovative features they bring to the world. I want to learn programming to enhance my understanding of technology not only on a hardware level but also on a software level. Although I haven’t decided on a specific career path yet, I’m confident that this program will help me discover the direction that best suits my passions and skills.

Ways ICT transforms the World:

  • Global Connectivity: ICT enables instant communication across the globe.
  • Education Access: Online learning platforms make education accessible to all.
  • Healthcare Improvement: Telemedicine allows remote medical consultations.
  • Economic Growth: E-commerce boosts businesses by reaching wider audiences.
  • Innovation Acceleration: ICT fosters new technologies and creative solutions.
